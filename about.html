About Us

Our journey, our commitment to vegetarian baking, and our love for Solan.

Crafting Delicious Memories

Welcome to Sagarika's Special. Our journey began with a simple belief: that every special occasion deserves a cake made with absolute care, passion, and personal customization. Based right here in Dev Bhoomi Green Valley Apartments, Deoghat, Solan, we take pride in being a home bakery that treats your celebrations as our own.

100% Veg (Eggless) Guarantee

We bake exclusively 100% vegetarian (eggless) cakes. To maintain this promise, we use dedicated egg-free utensils, pans, and mixing equipment. There is absolute peace of mind for you and your family that your custom cakes are completely veg, without compromising on texture, moistness, or rich gourmet flavors.

Our Quality & Hygiene Standard

Because we bake from a private home kitchen in small, handcrafted batches, we maintain rigorous hygiene controls. From sanitizing workspaces before preparation to sourcing premium dairy, fine chocolate, and real fruits, our cakes are baked fresh on order to guarantee top quality.
